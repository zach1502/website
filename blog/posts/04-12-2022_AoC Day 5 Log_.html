Stacks and Stacks | AoC Day 5

Posted on December 3rd, 2022

Today's question was about arranging stacks of crates and moving N crates at a time.

Like all the other early questions, it is still simple. A notable roadblock though was that the input was hard to parse in C++. That slowed me down a bit. Unlike the previous questions, there was two inputs I had to read in. The first was the starting stack contents and the second was the moves to make. But once I got the input parsed, the rest was easy. It was the simple pops and pushes to and from a stack.

Part 2's twist was that you can move multiple crates at a time and the order will stay the same. but all that took to solve was a buffer stack to hold the crates and then pop them back into the destination stack.

Part 1 took a speedy 15:39 minutes to complete thus getting me rank 1601! Part 2 took 15:48 minutes to complete thus getting me rank 1014! The best results so far!
