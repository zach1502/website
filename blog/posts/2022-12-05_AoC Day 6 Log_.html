Unique Window | AoC Day 6

Posted on December 5th, 2022

Today's question was about finding the first occurence of 4 unique letters in a long string.

This question was a noticable difficulty drop from the last few questions, it wasn't all that hard to solve. I used a for loop to grab substrings of length 4 and then naively checked if they were unique using 2 for-loops.

Part 2's twist was that I am now looking for 14 unique letters in a string. I simply changed a few numbers and I was done.
