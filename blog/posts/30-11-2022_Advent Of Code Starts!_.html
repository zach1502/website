Advent Of Code Starts! | Day 1

Posted on November 26th, 2022

Yes. I was going to study for my finals I said in the last post.

What did I do instead? I started Advent of Code.

I wanted to participate because I wanted to earn the coveted discord role @2022-Advent-of-Code-CHAMPION in the CSSS discord server. And also get an official letter of congratulations from the CSSS Executive team. I don't have many achievements so I thought I would aim for one.

I chose to do the challenge in C++. The question for day 1 gave a list of numbers and empty lines. You have to find the section of numbers whos sum is the largest. I had a bit of a shakey start because I forgot how to use stringstream. Otherwise it was one and done.

Part 2 asks you to find the maximum, 2nd maximum, and 3rd maximum sum of a section in the given blank-line separated list. This took very little thought to adjust my code and get the new answer.

Day 1 was a good start. I'm looking forward to the rest of the challenge. I finished part 1 in 7:09 and part 2 in 8:18. Earning me Rank 4018 for part 1 and Rank 2961 for part 2.
